Exercise Plans

Find exercise plans that suit your fitness goals.

Types of Exercise Plans

Beginner Plans

  • 30-Minute Walk: A simple daily walking routine to boost cardiovascular health.
  • Bodyweight Exercises: Push-ups, squats, and lunges to build strength.
  • Yoga Basics: Gentle yoga sessions to improve flexibility and relaxation.
  • Stretching Routine: A series of stretches to enhance flexibility and reduce soreness.
  • Dance Fitness: Fun dance routines to get moving while enjoying music.

Intermediate Plans

  • HIIT Workouts: High-Intensity Interval Training for efficient calorie burning.
  • Resistance Training: Weight lifting to build muscle and strength.
  • Running Program: Gradually increasing running distances to build endurance.
  • Circuit Training: Combining strength and cardio exercises for a full-body workout.
  • Boxing Fitness: Kickboxing or shadowboxing sessions for cardiovascular and muscular endurance.

Advanced Plans

  • CrossFit: A high-intensity fitness program combining strength and conditioning.
  • Marathon Training: A structured plan for preparing for long-distance running.
  • Powerlifting: Focused training to increase maximum strength in three lifts.
  • Triathlon Training: Preparing for swimming, biking, and running events.
  • Calisthenics: Advanced bodyweight exercises focusing on strength, balance, and flexibility.
