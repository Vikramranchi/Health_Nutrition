Recommended Daily Diet by Age Group

Discover the recommended calorie intake and ideal food types for various age groups to maintain a healthy lifestyle.

2-3 Years

  • Calories: 1,000–1,400 kcal/day
  • Foods: Fruits, whole grains, dairy, lean proteins
  • Recommended: Milk, yogurt, soft fruits, small servings of lean meats

4-8 Years

  • Calories: 1,200–1,800 kcal/day
  • Foods: Vegetables, fruits, dairy, protein
  • Recommended: Eggs, whole grains, leafy greens, fish

9-13 Years

  • Calories: 1,600–2,600 kcal/day
  • Foods: Protein, dairy, whole grains, fruits
  • Recommended: Chicken, legumes, low-fat cheese, nuts

14-18 Years

  • Calories: 1,800–3,200 kcal/day
  • Foods: Protein, vegetables, healthy fats, whole grains
  • Recommended: Fish, eggs, lean meats, nuts, seeds

19-30 Years

  • Calories: 2,000–3,000 kcal/day
  • Foods: Protein, vegetables, fruits, whole grains
  • Recommended: Lean meats, vegetables, fruits, whole grains, dairy

31-50 Years

  • Calories: 1,800–2,800 kcal/day
  • Foods: Vegetables, fruits, whole grains, lean proteins
  • Recommended: Low-fat dairy, poultry, fish, nuts

51+ Years

  • Calories: 1,600–2,400 kcal/day
  • Foods: Fiber-rich foods, lean proteins, dairy
  • Recommended: Vegetables, whole grains, fish, low-fat dairy
